About

As a dedicated and detail-oriented full stack web developer, I bring a strong foundation in modern web technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Node.js. My passion lies in building intuitive, responsive, and scalable applications that deliver real value to users. Through hands-on projects and continuous learning, I’ve developed a comprehensive understanding of both front-end and back-end development, with a strong focus on clean architecture and seamless user experience.

Web Developer Journey.

Prior to entering the tech industry, I served for many years as a qualified paramedic and firefighter. These high-stakes roles instilled in me a deep sense of responsibility, adaptability, and precision — qualities that naturally translate into my work as a developer. In emergency services, every second counts and every decision matters. That mindset now drives my approach to development: solving problems efficiently, writing dependable code, and collaborating effectively under pressure.

What sets me apart is the unique blend of real-world experience and technical expertise. My background has given me a deep appreciation for systems that are both robust and user-friendly, especially in critical environments. I approach every project with a mission-focused mentality — building applications that are not only functional and elegant but also meaningful. As I continue to grow as a full stack developer, I’m driven by a clear goal: to create technology that serves people and stands up to real-world challenges.
